IMAGE SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
========================

To display your profile image on the hero section, follow these steps:

1. Save your profile image with the filename: praneetha.jpg
   (or any JPG/PNG format)

2. Place it in the images/ folder:
   d:\DevOps-tools\Portfolio_website_Gitio\images\praneetha.jpg

3. The image will be automatically displayed in the hero section with:
   - Professional styling and border
   - Hover effects (scales up on hover)
   - Responsive design (adapts to mobile)
   - Orange glow effect matching your theme

RECOMMENDED IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Aspect Ratio: Square (1:1) or portrait (3:4)
- Min Size: 400x400px
- Max Size: 1000x1000px
- Background: Should have clear subject (like a professional photo)

CURRENT IMAGE PATH IN HTML:
src="images/praneetha.jpg"

If you use a different filename, update the src attribute in index.html line 71:
<img src="images/YOUR_IMAGE_NAME.jpg" alt="Praneetha - AI & Data Engineer">
